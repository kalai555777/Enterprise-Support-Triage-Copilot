# Tax, Currency, and Compliance

## Tax Determination
Tax is calculated from the billing address and applicable jurisdiction on the
invoice generation date. Customers must keep their billing address and any tax
identifiers current; changes after invoice generation apply only to future invoices.

## Tax-Exempt Customers
Customers with valid tax-exempt status upload their exemption certificate to the
billing settings. Once verified, tax is suppressed on subsequent invoices. The
certificate must be renewed before its expiry to avoid tax being reapplied.

## Currency
Invoices are issued in USD by default. Enterprise customers on annual contracts may
negotiate invoicing in a local currency; the exchange rate is fixed at contract
signing for the contract term to provide predictable budgeting.

## Compliance Records
ESTC retains billing records for the period required by applicable regulations.
Customers can export their invoice history as PDF or CSV from the billing dashboard
for their own accounting and audit needs.

## VAT and Reverse Charge
For applicable regions, a valid VAT identifier on file enables reverse-charge
handling, where the customer self-accounts for VAT and the invoice is issued without
it. Support verifies the VAT identifier format before enabling this treatment.
