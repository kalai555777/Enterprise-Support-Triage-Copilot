# Payment Methods

## Supported Methods
ESTC accepts major credit cards, ACH bank transfer for Enterprise customers, and
wire transfer for annual contracts. Growth customers must keep a valid credit card
on file for automatic monthly billing.

## Updating a Card
Customers update their payment method on the account billing settings page. A new
card is validated with a small temporary authorization that is reversed
immediately. The updated card becomes the default for the next billing cycle.

## Failed Payments
When a charge is declined, the billing system retries the charge after 24 hours and
again after 72 hours. If all retries fail, the invoice is marked overdue and the
dunning sequence begins. Persistent failures move the account toward Delinquent.

## Expired Cards
If a card on file expires before the next invoice, the system emails the technical
point of contact 14 days in advance. Refunds owed to an expired card are converted
into an account credit applied to the next invoice instead.

## Security
Card numbers are never stored on ESTC servers; they are tokenized by the payment
processor. Support specialists can see only the last four digits and the card brand,
never the full card number.
