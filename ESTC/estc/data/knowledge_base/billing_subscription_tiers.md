# Subscription Tiers and Entitlements

## Tier Overview
ESTC offers three subscription tiers: Free, Growth, and Enterprise. The tier on a
customer's record governs API rate limits, support SLA, and included seat count.

## Free
The Free tier is intended for evaluation. It includes a capped monthly API quota,
community support only, and no uptime SLA. Free accounts incur no charges and
cannot request refunds or credits.

## Growth
The Growth tier is billed monthly and includes a higher API quota, email support
with a next-business-day response target, and up to twenty-five seats. Overage
beyond the included quota is metered and itemized on the monthly invoice.

## Enterprise
The Enterprise tier includes negotiated annual pricing, a dedicated account
manager, a 99.9% uptime SLA, priority support, and single sign-on. Enterprise
customers may request service-level credits when an outage breaches the SLA.

## Upgrading and Downgrading
Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the remainder of the billing
cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle so the
customer retains paid entitlements they have already been charged for.
