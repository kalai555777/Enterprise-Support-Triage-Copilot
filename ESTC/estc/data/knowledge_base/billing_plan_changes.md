# Plan Changes and Proration

## Proration Rules
When a customer upgrades mid-cycle, the new plan price is prorated for the days
remaining in the current billing cycle and charged immediately. When a customer
downgrades, the change is deferred to the next cycle and a prorated credit may be
owed for unused premium entitlements.

## Seat Adjustments
Adding seats on Growth or Enterprise takes effect immediately and is prorated.
Removing seats takes effect at the next cycle. Seat counts are enforced at login;
exceeding the licensed seat count blocks new sign-ins until seats are freed or added.

## Annual to Monthly
Enterprise customers on annual contracts who switch to monthly billing do so only
at contract renewal. Switching early requires account-manager approval and may
forfeit negotiated annual discounts.

## Credits from Downgrades
A prorated credit from a downgrade is applied automatically to the next invoice. If
the customer cancels entirely before the next invoice, the outstanding credit is
refunded to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

## Verifying a Plan Change
Support specialists confirm a plan change by checking the subscription_tier field on
the customer record and the effective date logged in the billing history.
