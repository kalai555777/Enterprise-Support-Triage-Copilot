# Refunds and Credit Policy

## Overview
This document describes how refunds are requested, approved, and processed for
Enterprise, Growth, and Free-tier customers. Refunds apply to subscription
charges, prorated plan changes, and erroneous duplicate charges.

## Eligibility
A customer is eligible for a refund when one of the following conditions holds:

- A duplicate charge was applied to the same invoice within a single billing cycle.
- The customer downgraded mid-cycle and is owed a prorated credit.
- A service-level credit was approved by the account manager after an outage.

Free-tier accounts are never charged and therefore are not eligible for refunds.
Delinquent accounts must clear the outstanding balance before any refund is issued.

## Processing Time
Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after approval. The funds are
returned to the original payment method on file. If the original card has expired,
the refund is converted into an account credit applied to the next invoice.

## How to Request
Support specialists open a refund case, attach the disputed invoice ID, and route
it to the billing approver. Once approved, the billing system issues the refund and
emails a confirmation to the technical point of contact for the company.

## Common Questions
- "Where is my refund?" — Confirm the approval date; the 5 to 7 business day window
  starts from approval, not from the original request.
- "Can I get a refund on a Free plan?" — No, Free-tier usage incurs no charges.
