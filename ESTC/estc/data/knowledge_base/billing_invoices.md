# Invoices and Billing Cycles

## Billing Cycle
Invoices are generated on the first calendar day of each month for the prior
month's usage plus the recurring subscription fee. Enterprise customers may
negotiate annual invoicing; Growth customers are billed monthly by default.

## Reading an Invoice
Each invoice contains the company_id, the subscription_tier at the time of billing,
line items for base subscription and metered overage, applied credits, and the
total amount due. The invoice PDF is attached to the monthly billing email.

## Disputing a Charge
If a customer disputes a line item, the support specialist verifies the metered
usage against the platform's usage dashboard. Overage charges above the plan
allowance are itemized separately so they can be audited line by line.

## Payment Terms
Standard payment terms are net-30 for Enterprise and due-on-receipt for Growth.
An account becomes Delinquent when an invoice remains unpaid 15 days past its due
date, which can trigger a downgrade in service availability.

## Tax and Currency
Invoices are issued in USD by default. Tax is applied based on the billing address
on file. Customers can update their billing address and tax identifiers through the
account settings page before the invoice generation date.
